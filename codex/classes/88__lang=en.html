Abilities:

Manaflasks II
Utilizing elemental spells in battle will charge up to two Manaflasks, allowing you to unleash Manaflask abilities when fully charged. Exploiting elemental weaknesses in battle will charge Manaflasks faster.

Mana Siphon
There is a high chance that a skill or spell cast may not consume mana.
